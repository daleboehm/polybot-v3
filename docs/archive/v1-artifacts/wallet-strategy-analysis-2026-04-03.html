The only limitation is market liquidity / position sizing ceiling.
2. DCA into Conviction Theses Works
AML's $1.19M profit from a single political market (Viktor Orbán) demonstrates that patient dollar-cost-averaging into mispriced events at value prices ($0.20-$0.40) is viable. 994 trades in one market shows deterministic conviction, not random gambling. Only active sell-side trader in top 20 (83.6% buy, 16.4% sell). 167 unique markets across crypto + sports suggests market-making intent. Extreme position sizing variation ($11 to $1.3M) indicates tactical entry/exit, not conviction-based holding. Crypto's wider spreads make this viable.

Probability math: if you buy 20 different championships at $0.05, expect ~1-2 to resolve YES (10-20x payout), breaking even and generating 100-300% ROI on capital deployed.
5. Spreads Dominate Sports Whale Category
Multicolored-Self (#1, $5.39M) proves spreads are the highest-edge sports market type. Spread pricing has more error than moneylines due to lower liquidity. Top wallets hunt spreads at $0.45-$0.65 with massive sizing ($14-$18K avg).
