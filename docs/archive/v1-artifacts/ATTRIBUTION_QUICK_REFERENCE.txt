KEY METRICS
───────────

PER-STRATEGY BREAKDOWN:

• total_positions: Count of open positions in strategy
• total_capital_deployed: Sum of $amount_risked across open positions
• unrealized_pnl: Current P&L on open positions
• realized_pnl: Closed P&L from resolved trades
• total_pnl: unrealized + realized

• total_trades: Count of closed trades
• winning_trades: Trades with PnL > 0
• losing_trades: Trades with PnL < 0
• win_rate: (winning_trades / total_trades) × 100%
• avg_win: Mean PnL for winning trades
• avg_loss: Mean |PnL| for losing trades
• profit_factor: (gross_profit / gross_loss)

• max_single_loss: Worst trade outcome
• max_single_win: Best trade outcome
• std_dev_returns: Volatility of trade returns
• sharpe_ratio: (mean_return / std_dev) × √252 (annualized risk-adjusted return)

INTERPRETING SHARPE RATIO:
  Sharpe > 1.0:  Excellent risk-adjusted returns
  Sharpe 0.5-1:  Good risk-adjusted returns
  Sharpe 0-0.5:  Poor risk-adjusted returns
  Sharpe < 0:    Losing money (negative expected return)
